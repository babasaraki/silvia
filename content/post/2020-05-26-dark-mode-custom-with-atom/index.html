Where I started

With Alison Hill’s Up and Running with Blogdown post! Super helpful, though because I came to it 2.5 years late, it was more like ‘up and running with lots of water breaks’ because I had to stop and account for changes made to the Hugo Academic theme in the meantime. The going futher section specifically talks about customizing the out-of-the-box theme and Alison directly links to her custom css file, which I closely referred to when changing colors in my custom css/scss file.

Alison’s file helped me customize everything from text colors and fonts to alert colors and borders for Hugo Academic’s light mode.
